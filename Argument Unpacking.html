Argument Unpacking¶

We can use * operator to unpack the arguments out of a list, tuple and ** operator to unpack the arguments out of a dictionary.

def func(a, b, c):
    print(a, b, c)

list1 = [1, 2, 3]
tuple1 = (4, 5, 6)
dict1 = {'a': 7, 'b': 8, 'c': 9}

func(*list1)

1 2 3

func(*tuple1)

4 5 6

func(**dict1)

7 8 9
